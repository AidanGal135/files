Accounting & Auditing Services

Accounting & Auditing Services

Clean books month over month, and audit work that stands up to scrutiny from funders, lenders, and boards.

Numbers you can actually make decisions with

Financial statements are only useful if you trust them. David Evans leads a practice built around two things: books that are accurate every month, not just at year-end, and audit or review engagements that give boards, lenders, and funders real confidence in the numbers.

We work with a mix of privately held businesses, nonprofits, and associations — organizations where the financial statements aren't just paperwork, they're something a board or grantor actually reads.

What's included

Accounting

  • Reviews (limited assurance reports)
  • Compilations of GAAP and/or Federal Tax basis statements (no assurance reports)
  • Setup of accounting systems
  • Reconciling accounts
  • Bookkeeping services
  • Preparation of budgets & payroll

Auditing

Non-public Businesses — Nonprofit Organizations — 401(k) Plans

  • Dedicated partner to provide year to year continuity of service
  • Specialists such as actuaries or appraisers available as needed
  • Reduced cost by involving your staff when possible
  • Personalized suggestions for improvement of observed deficiencies
  • Reports for internal control are also available

How an engagement runs

1.

Scoping

We confirm what your board, lender, or grantor actually requires — compilation, review, or audit — before quoting anything.

2.

Fieldwork

Clear, upfront document requests and a fieldwork schedule that respects your team's time.

3.

Reporting

A finished report plus a plain-language walkthrough — and a management letter you can actually act on.
