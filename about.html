About the Firm

Built on names, not numbers.

Three partners, one manageable roster of clients, and a habit of picking up the phone.

Our Story

A firm-sized on purpose.

Russell, Evans & Thompson was founded around a straightforward frustration: too many good businesses were getting big-firm turnover and small-firm resources, without ever getting an actual relationship with the people handling their money.

So the firm was built the other way around. Matthew Russell, David Evans, and Thomas Thompson each lead their own practice area — tax, audit, and advisory — but every engagement is scoped so a partner is involved from the first meeting, not just the year-end signature.

That structure hasn't changed as the firm has grown. It's the reason clients tend to stay for decades, and the reason referrals still make up most of how new ones find us.
