Consulting Services

Advisory & consulting

For the decisions that don't come around every tax season — structuring, succession, and long-range strategy.

The conversations that happen once a decade

Some financial decisions repeat every year. Others — selling the business, bringing in a partner, planning a succession, restructuring an entity — happen once, and they're hard to undo. Thomas Thompson works with owners specifically on that second category, usually starting well before the decision is urgent.

Because the advisory practice sits inside the same firm as tax and audit, recommendations account for the full picture — not just the deal in front of you, but what it does to next year's return and the books behind it.

What's included

  • Advisory services for buying, selling, and creating a business
  • Personalized strategic planning
    • More efficient operations
    • Stronger financial structure
    • Improved accounting & administrative controls
  • Accounting system establishment
    • Manual & computerized processes
    • Personalized for your business's needs

How an engagement starts

1.

Discovery conversation

An unhurried talk about where the business is headed, not just what's on the balance sheet today.

2.

Options & modeling

We lay out realistic paths forward, with the numbers behind each one, not just a recommendation.

3.

Implementation support

Coordination with your attorney, banker, or successor so the plan survives contact with reality.
